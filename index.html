02 // FIELD VALIDATION

REAL-WORLD STRESS TEST

TEST ID: 001

PRODUCT PACKAGING

9.2 OUT OF 10
DUST/SCRATCH LOW LIGHTING PARTIAL OBSTRUCTION
SCENARIO

200L steel drums, 1000L IBC tanks, and cement bags in harsh site conditions.

✅ PASS (STRONG)

WHY IT WORKS

  • Bold DAM block is highly legible
  • Red “mix” creates fast brand recall
  • High contrast on white/metal

WATCH-OUTS

  • Symbol must not be printed too small
  • Red line risks loss if scaled below threshold
RECOMMENDATION Define a minimum symbol size rule. Create a symbol-only mark variant for tight spaces.
TEST ID: 002

SITE VISIBILITY

8.4 OUT OF 10
MOTION (60KM/H) DISTANCE (40M) GLARE/DIRT
SCENARIO

Viewed on moving trucks and plants by operators and engineers from distance.

⚠️ PASS (WITH CAVEAT)

STRENGTHS

  • DAM block is rock solid
  • Feels "technical" not decorative
  • Color contrast holds up well

WEAKNESSES

  • Abstract symbol slow to read from distance
  • Relies on familiarity, not instant shape recognition
RECOMMENDATION Prioritize wordmark for distance branding. Use symbol for close-range premium contexts (like Sika/MC).
