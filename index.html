ENGINEERED TRAJECTORY

FIG 1.1: TRAJECTORY VS CONSTRAINT

The DAMmix symbol is not a checkmark. It is a diagram of force and resistance.

  • The Red Axis (Limit): Represents the constraints of the project—seismic events, extreme weather, time pressure, and material thresholds.
  • The Blue Form (Trajectory): Represents the engineered solution. It does not stop at the limit. It is designed to pass through it, predictably and safely.
  • The Balance: We do not ignore limits. We design against them.
